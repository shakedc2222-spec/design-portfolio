Hi, I'm Shaked.

Brand & Visual Experience Designer.

I chose the world of design because it has always pulled me in from every direction—whether as a child looking to draw everywhere, or as a girl redesigning clothes for my dolls when I lost them. I have always looked for alternative solutions and noticed things others might have missed.

Design is the first thing people notice. Most don't think about it, but designers are the ones who shape the world we know today. Take Coca-Cola, for example: people immediately see the iconic red and the handwritten logo, but it goes far beyond that—it's everything around it, from the sound of the fizz that everyone knows to the entire experience. That is what gives a brand its identity, its life, and a home for others.

I love this ability to influence. To me, the magic happens when an idea born in my head becomes something tangible—flying on billboards, sitting on store shelves, or integrating into people's daily lives. Beyond aesthetics, in every project, I look for a value-add—whether social, economic, or emotional—something unique that comes from my own personal world.

In this portfolio, you will find projects where I gave brands life, connecting strategic thinking with creativity.
