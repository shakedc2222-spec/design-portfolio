Anila — Tradition Refined

Holistic wellness branding based on ancient tradition.

The Challenge

The wellness market is saturated with chemical solutions that create dependency. How do we design a brand that feels like a natural, accessible, and calming "anchor" in the daily race?

The Solution

Holistic branding based on ancient Indian tradition. The visual language combines soft, pleasant packaging, calming colors, and a design language that translates "calm" into a physical product. The brand offers solutions for focus, stress reduction, and sleep quality, with a marketing strategy based on content and accessible user experience.

Logo & Typography

The logo combines three symbols: wind, the scarf of Vayu (the Indian wind god), and a bird head symbolizing wind and movement. The typography was custom-built by constructing new letterforms from an existing typeface.

Product Line

Each color collection carries the same core range — diffuser, roll-on, cream, and spray — distinguished by its own scent story and palette.

Packaging & Product Range

Three product lines — Nest, Root, and Bloom — each with its own color story: Nest ("relax your body, reset your mind"), Root ("sharpen your focus, anchor your energy"), and Bloom ("awaken your senses, brighten your day").
