Rami Fortis — Influencer Kit

A "Monster Cat" themed kit capturing a musician's wild, iconic energy.

The Challenge

Design an influencer kit for musician Rami Fortis that captures his wild and iconic energy in an experiential way.

The Solution

A "Monster Cat" themed kit designed as a refrigerator. The experience includes an original card game, branded accessories, and drinks, giving influencers a glimpse into Fortis's unique "cat world" with a bold, colorful design that highlights his distinctive DNA.

The Kit

The Card Game

An original card game — complete with bottle coasters, a branded tin case, and a full deck of question and task cards — designed to bring Fortis's bold, colorful "cat world" to life at the table.

Product Showcase

Branded treats extend the "cat world" into everyday products — a lemon cocktail and a savory snack, each carrying Fortis's bold, colorful identity.
